How to Get Started with Trezor


  • Grab Your Trezor Device

    If you don’t have a Trezor hardware wallet yet, you can purchase one from our official website. Once you’ve got your Trezor device, you’re ready to start.

  • Plug in Your Device

    Simply plug your Trezor device into your computer or mobile device using the included USB cable. It’s that easy.

  • Open Trezor Suite

    Trezor Suite is your all-in-one crypto management app. Download and install Trezor Suite on your computer or use it directly in your browser. Once installed, open Trezor Suite to begin.

  • Connect Your Wallet

    When you launch Trezor Suite, follow the on-screen instructions to connect your hardware wallet. This will securely pair your Trezor device with the Suite, giving you access to all of its features.

  • Start Managing Your Crypto

    From here, you can send and receive coins, track your portfolio, trade digital assets, or even make payments for everyday essentials. Every action you take within the app will require your explicit confirmation directly on your Trezor hardware wallet, ensuring your transactions are always secure.

Key Features of Trezor Suite


  • Send and Receive Coins Securely

    Trezor Suite allows you to easily send and receive cryptocurrency with the highest level of security. When you initiate a transaction, it will ask for your confirmation on your Trezor device, making sure that you are in control at all times.

  • Track Your Portfolio

    Keep an eye on your investments and portfolio performance with Trezor Suite’s intuitive dashboard. Whether you’re holding Bitcoin, Ethereum, or any other asset, you can track your holdings with ease.

  • Trade and Pay

    You can also trade assets or use your crypto to make purchases for daily essentials directly from the app. With Trezor Suite, accessing your funds and converting them into real-world value has never been more simple.

  • Maximum Security with Hardware Wallet Confirmation

    Every transaction, whether sending, receiving, or trading, will require your explicit confirmation on your Trezor hardware wallet. This adds an extra layer of protection against online threats and ensures that only you can authorize transactions.
